Michelin‑Style Beef Wellington (No Mushrooms)

Rapini (broccoli rabe) duxelles • Prosciutto • Savory crêpes • Butter puff pastry

Yield: 4–6 Prep: 1h 15m Cook: 40m Total: ~2h 40m Target: 48–50°C (118–122°F)

Ingredients

Beef & Assembly

  • 1 center‑cut beef tenderloin (1–1.2 kg), trimmed
  • Fine sea salt & freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp neutral oil (grapeseed/canola)
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter + 2 sprigs thyme + 1 crushed garlic clove
  • Dijon mustard (optional; thin brush on seared beef)
  • 8–10 thin slices prosciutto di Parma
  • 2 thin savory crêpes (recipe below)
  • 1 sheet high‑quality butter puff pastry
  • Flour (for dusting)
  • Egg wash: 1 yolk + 1 tbsp cream
  • Flaky salt (finishing)

Rapini Duxelles (Mushroom‑Free)

  • 1 large bunch rapini / broccoli rabe (450–500 g)
  • 1 small shallot, very finely minced
  • 1 clove garlic, micro‑minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil + 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Zest of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • White pepper or black pepper, to taste
  • Pinch red pepper flakes (optional)

Savory Crêpes

  • 1 large egg
  • 60 g all‑purpose flour
  • 150 ml whole milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp neutral oil

Directions

  1. Savory crêpes: Whisk egg, flour, milk, salt, and oil into a thin batter. Rest 20 minutes.
  2. Savory crêpes: Cook 2 paper‑thin crêpes in a non‑stick pan. Cool completely.
  3. Rapini duxelles: Trim rapini; blanch in well‑salted boiling water 45 seconds; shock in ice bath.
  4. Rapini duxelles: Squeeze extremely dry; finely chop to a spreadable duxelles‑like texture.
  5. Rapini duxelles: Sweat shallot in olive oil + butter over low heat (no color). Add garlic 30 seconds.
  6. Rapini duxelles: Add rapini; cook 10–12 minutes, stirring, until very dry and concentrated. Finish with lemon zest, Dijon, pepper, optional chili flakes. Cool completely.
  7. Beef: Season generously. Sear in very hot oil 30–45 seconds per side. Add butter, thyme, garlic; baste briefly.
  8. Beef: Remove; optionally brush a thin layer of Dijon. Chill uncovered 20–30 minutes until cold/firm.
  9. Wrap: Lay plastic wrap. Overlap prosciutto into a rectangle; place crêpes on top; spread a thin, even layer of cooled rapini duxelles.
  10. Wrap: Place chilled beef in center; roll tightly using plastic wrap; twist ends to compress. Chill 30–45 minutes.
  11. Pastry: Roll puff pastry to ~3 mm. Unwrap beef and wrap in pastry; seal seam underneath; trim excess. Chill 15–20 minutes.
  12. Finish & bake: Brush with egg yolk + cream. Lightly score decor (shallow only). Bake at 200°C / 400°F (center rack) 35–40 minutes.
  13. Doneness: Target 48–50°C (118–122°F) internal for medium‑rare. Rest uncovered 10–12 minutes. Finish with flaky salt; slice.

Pro tip: For the cleanest slices, use a serrated knife and wipe the blade between cuts.
