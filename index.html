Michelin Style Beef Wellington No Mushrooms

Rapini duxelles, prosciutto, savory crepes, butter puff pastry

Yield 4 to 6 Prep 1 hour 15 minutes Cook 40 minutes Total about 2 hours 40 minutes Target 48 to 50 C internal
Import tip for Samsung Food: open Samsung Food, go to Saved, tap plus, choose Save recipe link, then paste your GitHub Pages URL.

Ingredients

Beef and assembly

  • Beef tenderloin, salt, pepper
  • Neutral oil, butter, thyme, garlic
  • Optional Dijon mustard
  • Prosciutto, savory crepes, puff pastry
  • Flour, egg yolk and cream, flaky salt

Rapini duxelles

  • Rapini, shallot, garlic
  • Olive oil and butter
  • Lemon zest, Dijon, pepper, optional chili

Savory crepes

  • Egg, flour, milk, salt, oil

Directions

  1. Savory crepes: Whisk batter and rest 20 minutes.
  2. Savory crepes: Cook very thin crepes and cool completely.
  3. Rapini duxelles: Blanch, shock, squeeze dry, and finely chop.
  4. Rapini duxelles: Sweat shallot, add garlic, cook rapini until very dry. Season and cool.
  5. Beef: Sear quickly, butter baste briefly, then chill until cold and firm.
  6. Wrap: Prosciutto plus crepes plus rapini layer. Roll tight and chill.
  7. Pastry: Wrap in puff pastry, seal seam under, chill again.
  8. Bake: Egg wash, light scoring, bake 200 C for 35 to 40 minutes.
  9. Rest: Rest uncovered 10 to 12 minutes and slice cleanly.
