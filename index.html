Michelin‑Style Beef Wellington (No Mushrooms)

Rapini (broccoli rabe) duxelles • Prosciutto • Savory crêpes • Butter puff pastry

Yield: 4–6 Prep: 1h 15m Cook: 40m Total: ~2h 40m Target: 48–50 C (118–122 F)

Ingredients

Beef & Assembly

  • 1 center‑cut beef tenderloin (1–1.2 kg), trimmed
  • Fine sea salt & freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp neutral oil (grapeseed/canola)
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter + thyme + 1 crushed garlic clove
  • Dijon mustard (optional; thin brush on seared beef)
  • 8–10 thin slices prosciutto
  • 2 thin savory crêpes
  • 1 sheet butter puff pastry
  • Egg wash: 1 yolk + 1 tbsp cream
  • Flour (for dusting) + flaky salt (finishing)

Rapini Duxelles

  • 1 bunch rapini (450–500 g)
  • 1 small shallot, very finely minced
  • 1 clove garlic, micro‑minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil + 1 tbsp butter
  • Zest of 1/2 lemon + 1 tsp Dijon
  • Pepper; optional chili flakes

Savory Crêpes

  • 1 egg, 60 g flour, 150 ml milk, pinch salt, 1 tsp oil

Directions

  1. Savory crêpes: Whisk egg, flour, milk, salt, and oil into a thin batter. Rest 20 minutes.
  2. Savory crêpes: Cook 2 paper‑thin crêpes in a non‑stick pan. Cool completely.
  3. Rapini duxelles: Trim rapini; blanch 45 seconds; shock in ice bath.
  4. Rapini duxelles: Squeeze extremely dry; finely chop.
  5. Rapini duxelles: Sweat shallot; add garlic 30 seconds.
  6. Rapini duxelles: Cook rapini 10–12 minutes until very dry. Finish with lemon zest, Dijon, pepper; cool completely.
  7. Beef: Season; sear 30–45 seconds per side. Briefly butter‑baste with thyme/garlic.
  8. Beef: Optional Dijon brush; chill uncovered 20–30 minutes.
  9. Wrap: Prosciutto + crêpes + thin rapini layer; roll tight around beef. Chill 30–45 minutes.
  10. Pastry: Wrap in puff pastry; seal seam underneath. Chill 15–20 minutes.
  11. Bake: Egg wash; score. Bake 200 C / 400 F for 35–40 minutes.
  12. Rest: Rest uncovered 10–12 minutes. Slice with a serrated knife.
